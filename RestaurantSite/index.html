Our Story

Dino's diner is a family owned and operated establishment named after the legendary Michael "Dino" Donahue. Featuring delicous produce from local farms and the freshest meats, our master chef provides you with an unforgettable dining experience.

Founded in 2004, Dino's brings rustic comfort food to the modern era. We believe the best food starts with the best ingredients. Our menu is hand-crafted from the bottom up, with fresh breads made every morning and only the most select cuts of meat prepared in the moment.

We are sure Dino's will be your new favorite spot and we look forward to giving you a dining experience to remember!
